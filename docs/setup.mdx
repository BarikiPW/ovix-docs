title: Setup
---

1.  Buy a license. You can do so [here](https://docs.ovix.one/resellers).
2.  Activate your license on [ovix.one](https://dash.ovix.one/).
    - Create an account
    - Then paste your license and click "Redeem".
3.  Download and install [VC Redist](https://aka.ms/vs/16/release/vc_redist.x64.exe). Also available on the dashboard.
4.  Download the [OVIX Loader](https://dash.ovix.one).
5.  Add an [antivirus exclusion](https://support.microsoft.com/en-gb/help/4028485/windows-10-add-an-exclusion-to-windows-security) to the unziped files. [Click here to open the Exclusions](ms-settings:windowsdefender).
6.  Launch GTA and load story mode
7.  When fully loaded in, launch the OVIX Loader and connect using your credentials.
8.  Press the inject button. This will inject the menu into your game.
9.  If everything went well pressing F4 should make the menu appear ingame
10. Enjoy!
